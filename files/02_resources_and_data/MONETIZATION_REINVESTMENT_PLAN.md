## 💵 REVENUE STREAMS (Choose 1-3)

### 1. **YouTube AdSense** (Passive, Highest at Scale)

**How It Works:**
- YouTube shows ads on your videos
- You get ~40% of ad revenue
- Payment: Monthly to bank account

**Requirements:**
- 1,000 subscribers
- 4,000 watch hours (past 12 months)
- Video must be >60 seconds (Shorts are ~30s, so this is a problem)

**Wait... Shorts Issue!**
Shorts don't easily hit 60-second watch hour requirement.

**Solution:** Mix short + long-form content
- YouTube Shorts: 30 seconds (growth, engagement)
- YouTube Videos: 8-10 minutes (same topic, more depth, gets watch hours)

**Example Split:**
- Monday: Shorts only (4 videos)
- Tuesday-Thursday: 1 video (10 min) + 3 shorts
- Friday: 1 compilation video (15 min)

**Revenue Potential:**
```
5,000 views/month → $10-30/month
20,000 views/month → $50-150/month
50,000 views/month → $150-400/month
100,000+ views/month → $300-1,000+/month
```

### 2. **YouTube Partner Program (Sponsorships)**

**How It Works:**
- Brands pay you directly to mention products
- You read sponsor message in video
- Payment: Direct to you

**Requirements:**
- 10,000+ subscribers
- Active channel (regular uploads)
- Good engagement rate

**How to Find Sponsors:**
1. Join networks:
   - GRIN: https://grin.co
   - AspireIQ: https://aspireiiq.com
   - Influee: https://influee.com

2. Direct outreach:
   - Email brands relevant to psychology/productivity
   - Nootropics companies
   - Self-help course creators
   - Productivity app makers

**Revenue Potential:**
```
10,000 subs: $500-2,000 per video
50,000 subs: $2,000-5,000 per video
100,000+ subs: $5,000-20,000+ per video
```

### 3. **Affiliate Marketing** (Best for Psychology/Productivity Niche)

**How It Works:**
- Recommend products in video descriptions
- People click your link → Buy product
- You get commission (5-50%)

**Best Affiliate Programs:**
- Amazon Associates (all products, 3-5% commission)
- Skillshare (psychology courses, $15+ per signup)
- Udemy (courses, $15-30 per sale)
- Nootropics brands (40-50% commission)
- Productivity tools (20-30% commission)

**How to Set Up:**
1. Join Amazon Associates (free)
2. Create link: https://amazon.com/?tag=YOUR_TAG
3. Add link in video description
4. YouTube detects clicks → You earn

**Revenue Potential:**
```
1,000 views/month → $20-50/month
10,000 views/month → $200-500/month
50,000+ views/month → $1,000-3,000+/month
```

**Create & Sell:**

**Platforms:**

**How to Promote:**

**Revenue Potential:**
```
100 sales/month @ $20 = $2,000/month
1,000 sales/month @ $20 = $20,000/month
(After platform fees: 85-95% goes to you)
```

### 5. **Email List + Newsletter** (Future Revenue)

## 🛑 DON'T DO THIS (Common Mistakes)

**❌ Mistake 1: Monetize Too Early**
- Ad networks have minimums (1,000 subs, 4,000 watch hours)
- Rushing ruins content quality
- Focus on growth FIRST, money LATER

**❌ Mistake 2: Sponsor Bad Products**
- People trust you = Damage if you promote trash
- Vet sponsors carefully
- Only promote what you actually like

**❌ Mistake 3: Overload Videos with Links**
- Too many calls-to-action = Low conversions
- 1 main CTA per video is optimal
- Don't be salesy

**❌ Mistake 4: Ignore Analytics**
- Don't know which topics convert
- Don't optimize for revenue
- Track: Views, watch %, engagement, conversions

**❌ Mistake 5: Diversify Too Early**
- Master 1 thing (YouTube Shorts) first
- Then expand platforms
- Then add products
- Spread attention = Spread thin
